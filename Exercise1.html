    Cooking instruction

    1. For the steak we will be using, kosher salt,black pepper, garlic , oilive oil, butter, fresh thyme and rosemary
    2. Apply Olilive oil,kosher salt and black pepper to the steak and make sure the steak is at room temperature
    3. Gently crush the garlic with the back of the knife
    4. Apply oil to the pan then add the crushed garlic
    5. once the pan is hot put the steak in make sure the pan is on high heat
    6. Once the steak gets some colour add the thyme and rosemary and add some butter to the pan allowing the butter to carmelized for moe flavour
    7. After that turn the heat down
    8. Since we are waiting for the steak to cook cut some potatoes into long shapes
    9. After that fly the potatoes into oil and deep fry them for 1 minute and then take them out deep fry them again at a high temp to get that golden brown colour
    10. Once they are golden brown take out and season them with salt as soon as they get out of the hot oil
    11. Once the butter of the steak is brown make sure to keep turning your steak allowing it to absorb all that falvour from the butter and the fresh herbs
    12. Once the steak is done cooking turn off the heat and let rhe steak rest in the pan for 5 minutes
    13. When the steak is doen resting its time for you to dig in and enjoy your steak and frites
